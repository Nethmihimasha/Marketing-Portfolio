Marketing Video Script
Project Hazel – AI Companion Robot
Overview: Developed a marketing video script for Hazel, an AI-powered companion robot designed to improve productivity, focus, and well-being for students and young professionals.

Objective: To create an engaging and emotionally compelling script that clearly communicates Hazel’s features while building a strong human connection with the audience.

Concept & Strategy: The concept focuses on positioning Hazel as more than a device a personal companion. The narrative highlights real-life student struggles such as distraction, lack of focus, and burnout, and presents Hazel as a solution through storytelling.

Key Elements: Strong hook to capture attention within the first few seconds, problem-solution storytelling structure, feature integration (focus tracking, reminders, music control), conversational and relatable tone.

Outcome: A structured, ready-to-produce video script designed to increase audience engagement and clearly communicate product value.
